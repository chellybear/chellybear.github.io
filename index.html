About




Hello, I'm Rachel!
I'm a student and digital artist living in Orange County, California. I'm currently majoring in Computer Science/Engineering.

Currently, I'm using resources like Udemy, FreeCodeCamp, and edX to improve my skills when I'm not taking classes. I also like to try and reverse-engineer sites I've come across online, in the hopes that I'll learn something new on the way!

My goal is to learn UI/UX development and design, since I love art as much as I love programming. I hope to use the skills I pick up to help design responsive UIs and apps for non-profits! :)
